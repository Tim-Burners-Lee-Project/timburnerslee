What Can a Website Do?

Websites are tools that connect people, information, and services across the world.

Sharing Information

News, school resources, tutorials, and knowledge are all accessible instantly.

Example: Wikipedia, BBC News

Communication

People can communicate instantly through messaging, email, and video calls.

Example: social media, online chats

Entertainment

Websites provide videos, music, and games.

Example: streaming platforms and online games

Online Services

You can shop, book tickets, and manage finances online.

Example: Amazon, online banking
