What Can a Website Do?

Websites are powerful tools used every day to share, connect, and provide services worldwide.

Sharing Information

Websites allow instant access to knowledge.

Examples include news, school resources, and tutorials.

This makes learning faster and more accessible.

Global Communication

People can communicate instantly across the world.

Email, messaging apps, and video calls all rely on websites.

This has made the world more connected than ever.

Entertainment

Websites provide videos, music, and games.

Streaming services and online gaming are hugely popular.

Entertainment is now available anytime, anywhere.

Online Services

You can shop, book tickets, and manage money online.

Businesses use websites to sell products and services.

This has changed how people shop and interact with companies.

Education

Online courses and learning platforms are widely used.

Students can learn from anywhere in the world.

Education is no longer limited to classrooms.

Why It Matters

Websites are essential in modern life.

They influence communication, business, and learning.

Almost everything today involves the web in some way.
