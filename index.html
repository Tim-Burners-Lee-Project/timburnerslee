What Can a Website Do?

Websites are interactive tools that allow people to share information, communicate, and use services.

They are a huge part of everyday life, from education to entertainment and business.

Sharing Information

Websites can display text, images, and data instantly.

This allows people to learn new things, read news, and access resources from anywhere.

For example, online encyclopedias and news sites are used daily by millions of people.

Playing Audio

Websites can play sound such as music, podcasts, or sound effects.

This is used in streaming platforms and games.

You can press play to hear an example.

Playing Video

Websites can stream videos for entertainment and education.

This is how platforms like YouTube work.

User Input

Websites can take input from users, such as forms and searches.

This is used for login systems, searches, and surveys.

Interactivity

Websites respond to user actions like clicks and scrolling.

Watch this text change colour!

This makes websites dynamic and engaging.

Online Services

Websites allow people to shop, book tickets, and manage money.

Businesses use websites to sell products and connect with customers.

This has transformed how people interact with companies.

Why It Matters

Websites combine information, media, and interaction in one place.

They are essential for communication, learning, and daily life.

As technology improves, websites continue to become more powerful.
