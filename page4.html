What Is He Doing Now?

Tim Berners-Lee is still working to improve the web.

Open Web

He believes the web should be free and accessible to everyone.

W3C

He founded the World Wide Web Consortium to set web standards.

Privacy & Data

He is working on projects that give people more control over their data.

The Future

His goal is a safer, more trustworthy internet for everyone.
